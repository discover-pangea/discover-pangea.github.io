About Us

Pangea was a supercontinent during the late Paleozoic era and it was comprised of all the continents. Our idea is that the world is one and we can discover it together.

Discover Pangea, LLC is a USA based travel company that helps tour operators from other countries develop and run successful student group trips.

Discover Pangea was created with the purpose to combine educational, personal development, career path and travel in one life-changing bundle. Our goal is helping young people discover the world, finding their passion and purpose.

Mission

Partner with tour operators from around the world to design and operate meaningful group trips that allow students to experience the world.

Vision

Raise the new generation of Global Citizens with a broad perspective, confidence, and desire to make the world a better place.

If you have a similar mission and vision, if you set education and development at the top of your priorities list, then we will be a good match! If you and your organization believe that our youth deserve a better future, let’s team up and help them explore the world safely and comfortably.

Our Story

Company’s values were established by the founder and owner, Anna Aguilar.

Anna has a Bachelor degree in ESOL and 6 years of experience in education. 8 years ago, Anna moved from Russia to Orlando, FL - one of the top tourist destination in the USA. She decided to find a way to combine her passion for education and travel. For the past 6 years, Anna has been working with student groups from India, Pakistan, Russia, Australia, Turkey, China, and UAE. She also arranged trips for students from the USA to Germany, Spain, and England.
