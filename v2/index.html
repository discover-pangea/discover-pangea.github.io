About Us

Pangea was a supercontinent during the late Paleozoic era comprised of the seven continents we are all familiar with today. They may appear separated but we still see the world as one that we can discover together.

Discover Pangea, LLC is a travel company based in the United States of America. We are dedicated to helping tour operators from other countries to develop and run successful student group trips.

Discover Pangea's goal is to help young people from around the world discover their passion and purpose by combining educational activities and travel into one potentially life-changing experience benefitting both their personal and career growth.

Mission

We partner with tour operators from around the world to design and operate meaningful group trips that allow students to experience the world.

Vision

We are helping raise the new generation of Global Citizens with a broad perspective, confidence, and desire to make the world a better place.

If you have a similar mission and vision, if you set education and development at the top of your priorities list, then we will be a good match! If you and your organization believe that our youth deserve a better future, let’s team up and help them explore the world safely and comfortably.

Take every opportunity to travel, near or far. Notice everything, big or small.

Our Story

Company’s values were established by the founder and owner, Anna Aguilar.

Discover Pangea is a way to combine her passion for education and travel. For the past 6 years, Anna has been working with student groups from India, Pakistan, Russia, Australia, Turkey, China, and UAE. She also arranged trips for students from the USA to Germany, Spain, and England. A bachelor degree in ESOL and diverse experience in education helps Anna add educational value to every trip. Some of the achievements include bringing first ever group to Kennedy Space Center’s International camp and first Brazilian group to Full Sail Game Design summer program. Creating long-lasting partnerships with educational institutions in Orlando (Full Sail, KEF Summer Camp, YMCA, Synapse Learning Center) and building friendships with many vendors and suppliers in the USA.
